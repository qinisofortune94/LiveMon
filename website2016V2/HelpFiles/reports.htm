Reports

This section of IP Mon displays the information that it has stored in table form. This information is what IP Mon uses to create the graphs, in the graphs section. By displaying it in table form, it allows you to view exact numerical values. This information is very similar to what you would export, in the export section of IP Mon.

To generate a report, simply check the boxes of the sensors you wish to receive information on, provide a start and end date and the information will be displayed for you.
