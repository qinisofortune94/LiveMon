Graphs

The graph section is where the IP Mon displays all your information in graphical format. This is the easiest way to view the data, using line or bar graphs as well as distinguishable colours.

Simply check the boxes of the sensors you wish to receive information on, provide a start and end date and then click on [Generate] to view the graphs. The default range allows you to specify certain time periods for the graph.
