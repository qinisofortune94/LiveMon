Edit Alerts

On occasion, you may want to edit an alert or delete it altogether. This section of IP Mon allows you to go back into the alerts and change them to suite you.

By selecting one of the alerts from this table, it will allow you to edit the details of the alert. You did this in much the same way as you added the initial alert.

This bar appears at the bottom of the edit alerts page, and allows you to update or delete various parts of your alert.
